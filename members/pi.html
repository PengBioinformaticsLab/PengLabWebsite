Dr. Gang Peng

Indiana University School of Medicine (since 09/01/2022)

Biography

Dr. Gang Peng is the Principal Investigator of the Peng Bioinformatics Lab. His research focuses on computational genomics, epigenomics, and integrative analysis of large-scale biomedical data, with applications in aging, cancer, and population health.
